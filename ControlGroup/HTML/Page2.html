Before we begin the study, we want to give you more detailed information on how it will be structured.

First, we want to explore the radix sort algorithm by playing with it. You will be presented five problems, and your task is going to be to write down how you think the algorithm could work to sort the presented arrays. Note that the input fields will be disabled after your first input! Therefore, we advise you to think carefully before giving an answer because you can not change it afterward in this part of the study.

For the second part of the study, we will give you a detailed description of the radix sort algorithm. Your task will then be to apply the newly gained knowledge to solve five more problems.

Afterward, you fill out a questionnaire on your experience.
